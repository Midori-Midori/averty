Our Mission: Achieve Zero Highway Pileups

Freeway rear-end collisions are uniquely dangerous due to the velocities involved. When one vehicle decelerates abruptly, the vehicles behind it form a compression wave. If warning arrives too late, a multi-vehicle pileup is inevitable.

  • Primary Target: Eliminate hidden tailback surprise factors.
  • Network Synergy: Coordinated alerts turn vehicles ahead into active warning nodes for those behind.
  • Localized Reach: Precision alerts targeting only the lane and heading affected.

How We Compute Deceleration Metrics

Averty does not rely on manual user feedback or reports. The system reads real-time velocity changes and calculates deceleration vectors (G-force).

  • Abrupt Deceleration Threshold: Triggers alerts automatically when deceleration exceeds 0.4g.
  • Spatial Indexing: Employs high-speed spatial indexing to resolve affected vehicles within 15 milliseconds.
  • Predictive Jam Warnings: Predicts where the tail of the traffic queue will sit when approaching vehicles arrive.

Cognitive Load Optimization

Standard navigation apps show maps filled with icons, streets, and menus, overloading a driver's attention. Averty is designed to minimize cognitive processing time.

  • Color Coding: Instant subconscious recognition (Green, Yellow, Orange, Red).
  • Full-screen Flash: Perceived in peripheral vision without requiring the driver to look directly at the phone screen.
  • Siren Acoustics: Distinct sound profiles tailored to urgency levels.
